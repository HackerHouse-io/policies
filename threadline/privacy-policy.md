# Threadline — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

Threadline is a daily word puzzle that runs entirely on Reddit's Developer
Platform (Devvit). It is designed to collect as little as possible. **There is no
external backend, no third-party analytics, and no advertising.** All game data is
stored within Reddit's managed infrastructure.

## What we store

Threadline stores only what it needs to run the game, keyed to the anonymous user
identifier Reddit provides to the app. We never see your password or account
credentials.

- **Your Reddit username** — to display you on the leaderboard.
- **Daily puzzle progress** — clues revealed, guesses, solved/failed status,
  score, and solve time, so the game works and can't be replayed for extra points.
- **Aggregate stats** — your streak, total solves, and best score.
- **Leaderboard entries** — your score and solve time for the daily and all-time
  boards.
- **Inventory & purchase history** — which extras you own (hints, streak freezes,
  supporter badge) and the Reddit order IDs needed to deliver them and avoid
  double-granting.
- **Reminder opt-in** — only if you choose to turn on the daily reminder.

Per-day data (daily progress, daily leaderboard, solve times) expires
automatically after roughly 40 days. Aggregate stats, inventory, and the all-time
leaderboard persist so your streak and history survive.

## What we do NOT collect

- No email address.
- No real name, phone number, location, or device identifiers.
- **No payment details.** Reddit Gold purchases are handled entirely by Reddit;
  the app only receives an order ID and which product was purchased.
- No third-party analytics, trackers, ad networks, or external servers.
- No reading of your posts, comments, or activity outside this app.

## Reminders

The daily reminder is **opt-in**. If you enable it, the app sends a Reddit
notification when a new puzzle is live and you haven't solved it yet. You can turn
it off any time in the app, and no further reminders are sent.

## Sharing

When you tap "share," the app posts a **spoiler-free** comment (your score, solve
time, and streak — never the answer) attributed to your username on the daily
puzzle post. Sharing is always an explicit action you choose to take.

## Your data and deletion

- Game data is tied to your Reddit account within this app only.
- Per-day data expires on its own (~40 days).
- A subreddit moderator can reset a player's current-day progress from the mod
  menu. To request removal of persistent data (stats/history), contact the
  moderators of r/ThreadlineGame.
- Removing or uninstalling the app from a subreddit removes its stored data for
  that install, per Reddit's platform data handling.

## Reddit's role

Threadline runs on Reddit and relies on Reddit for accounts, hosting, and payment
processing. Your use of Reddit is also governed by the
[Reddit User Agreement](https://www.redditinc.com/policies/user-agreement) and the
[Reddit Privacy Policy](https://www.reddit.com/policies/privacy-policy).

## Contact

Questions about data handling: reach the moderators of **r/ThreadlineGame**. This
policy may be updated as the app evolves; material changes will be noted in the
subreddit.
