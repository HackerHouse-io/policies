## Description of Service

FreeSpace is a photo and video management application designed to help you organize and free up storage space on your iOS device. The App provides tools to:

- Identify and remove duplicate photos and videos
- Find and group similar photos
- Detect low-quality images
- Compress large video files
- Manage screenshots and large videos

The App requires access to your photo library to function. By granting this permission, you authorize the App to:

- Read and analyze your photos and videos
- Display thumbnails and previews within the App

Deleted items are moved to the Recently Deleted album in Photos, where they remain for 30 days before permanent deletion by iOS.
