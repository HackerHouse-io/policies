# FreeSpace — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Introduction

FreeSpace ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the FreeSpace mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Photo Library Data

The App accesses your device's photo library to provide its core functionality. We access:

- Photo and video metadata (file size, creation date, dimensions, duration)
- Image content for analysis (duplicate detection, quality assessment, similarity grouping)
- Your keep/delete decisions within the App

**This data is processed entirely on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.**

### Usage Data

We do not use any third-party analytics services. No usage data is collected or transmitted to external servers.

### Purchase Information

If you make in-app purchases, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store and RevenueCat. We receive:

- Confirmation of purchase (subscription status)
- Anonymous transaction identifiers
- Subscription expiration dates

We do not receive or store your payment details, credit card information, or Apple ID.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the App's photo management features
- To process and manage your subscription (if applicable)
- To improve the App based on anonymous usage patterns
- To diagnose technical issues and fix bugs
- To communicate important updates about the App

## Data Storage and Security

Your photo library data is processed and stored locally on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your photos or videos on any external servers.

No analytics data is collected or transmitted to third-party services.

Subscription data is securely managed by RevenueCat and Apple.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **Apple App Store** — Payment processing for in-app purchases
- **RevenueCat** — Subscription management and validation

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

## Data Retention

Local data (your decisions, preferences, and cached thumbnails) remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data.

Subscription records are retained as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Revoke photo library access at any time through iOS Settings
- Delete all local App data by uninstalling the App
- Request information about data we hold (contact us below)

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the App and updating the effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email listed on the App Store.
