# Pathline — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

Pathline is a daily path-drawing puzzle that runs entirely on Reddit's Developer
Platform (Devvit). It is designed to collect as little as possible. **There is no
external backend, no third-party analytics, and no advertising.** All game data is
stored within Reddit's managed infrastructure.

## What we store

Pathline stores only what it needs to run the game, keyed to the anonymous user
identifier Reddit provides to the app. We never see your password or account
credentials.

- **Your Reddit username** — to display you on the leaderboard and your profile.
- **Daily puzzle progress** — your status (playing/solved/failed), hints used,
  start time, solve time, backtrack count, and result category, so the game works
  and can't be replayed for extra points.
- **Aggregate stats** — your current and longest streak, total solves, flawless and
  clean solve counts, best solve time, practice solves, and points.
- **Leaderboard entries** — your solve time, result category, and points for the
  daily and all-time boards.
- **Public profile** — your username, a cached copy of your Reddit Snoovatar (so the
  leaderboard loads quickly), supporter status, streaks, and earned milestone badges.
- **Inventory & purchase history** — which extras you own (bonus hints, streak
  freezes, supporter badge) and the Reddit order IDs needed to deliver them and
  avoid double-granting.
- **Reminder opt-in** — only if you choose to turn on the daily reminder.

Per-day data (daily progress, daily leaderboard, solve times) expires automatically
after roughly 40 days. Cached Snoovatar images refresh about once a day. Aggregate
stats, inventory, and the all-time leaderboard persist so your streak and history
survive.

## What we do NOT collect

- No email address.
- No real name, phone number, location, or device identifiers.
- **No payment details.** Reddit Gold purchases are handled entirely by Reddit; the
  app only receives an order ID and which product was purchased.
- No third-party analytics, trackers, ad networks, or external servers.
- No reading of your posts, comments, or activity outside this app.

## Reminders

The daily reminder is **opt-in**. If you enable it, the app sends a Reddit
notification when a new puzzle is live and you haven't solved it yet. You can turn
it off any time in the app, and no further reminders are sent.

## Sharing

When you tap "share," the app posts a **spoiler-free** comment (your solve time,
result, and streak — never the path or solution) attributed to your username on the
daily puzzle post. Sharing is always an explicit action you choose to take.

## Your data and deletion

- Game data is tied to your Reddit account within this app only.
- Per-day data expires on its own (~40 days).
- A subreddit moderator can reset a player's current-day progress from the mod menu.
  To request removal of persistent data (stats/history), contact the moderators of
  r/PathlineGame.
- Removing or uninstalling the app from a subreddit removes its stored data for that
  install, per Reddit's platform data handling.

## Reddit's role

Pathline runs on Reddit and relies on Reddit for accounts, hosting, and payment
processing. Your use of Reddit is also governed by the
[Reddit User Agreement](https://www.redditinc.com/policies/user-agreement) and the
[Reddit Privacy Policy](https://www.reddit.com/policies/privacy-policy).

## Contact

Questions about data handling: reach the moderators of **r/PathlineGame**. This
policy may be updated as the app evolves; material changes will be noted in the
subreddit.
