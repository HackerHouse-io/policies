# Ocean Drive Racing — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

Ocean Drive Racing is a pixel-art drag racing game that runs entirely on Reddit's
Developer Platform (Devvit). It is designed to collect as little as possible. **There
is no external backend, no third-party analytics, and no advertising.** All game data
is stored within Reddit's managed infrastructure.

## What we store

Ocean Drive Racing stores only what it needs to run the game, keyed to the anonymous
user identifier Reddit provides to the app. We never see your password or account
credentials.

- **Your Reddit username** — to display you on the leaderboards and your profile.
- **Your race results** — finish time, launch/shift/nitro ratings, top speed,
  execution score, and rewards earned, so the game works and races can be ranked.
- **Your player profile** — in-game cash and chips balances, owned cars and your
  active car, installed parts and your parts inventory, daily login streak, total
  races completed, best all-time time, Driver XP, campaign progress (missions, best
  times, medals), and your weekly league standing (division, Race Points,
  promotion/relegation history).
- **Daily replay input** — the launch, shift, and nitro timing of your best daily run,
  used to show a "ghost" of your run and to prevent replaying for extra points.
- **Leaderboard entries** — your time and Race Points on the daily, all-time, weekly
  league, and lifetime boards.
- **Cached usernames** — a stored copy of player usernames so leaderboards load
  quickly.

Per-day data (the daily leaderboard, your daily best run, daily replay input) expires
automatically after a short window. Aggregate stats, your profile, league standing,
and the all-time and lifetime boards persist so your progress and history survive.

## What we do NOT collect

- No email address.
- No real name, phone number, location, or device identifiers.
- **No payment details.** Ocean Drive Racing has **no real-money purchases** — all
  cash, chips, cars, parts, and crates are earned through gameplay and have no
  monetary value.
- No third-party analytics, trackers, ad networks, or external servers.
- No reading of your posts, comments, or activity outside this app.

## Sharing

Any time the app posts a comment on your behalf (for example, sharing a race result),
it is an **explicit action you choose to take** and is attributed to your username on
the game's post. The app does not post on your behalf without your action.

## Your data and deletion

- Game data is tied to your Reddit account within this app only.
- Per-day data expires on its own after a short window.
- A subreddit moderator can reset a player's current-day progress from the mod menu.
  To request removal of persistent data (profile/stats/history), contact the
  moderators of **r/OceanDriveRacing**.
- Removing or uninstalling the app from a subreddit removes its stored data for that
  install, per Reddit's platform data handling.

## Reddit's role

Ocean Drive Racing runs on Reddit and relies on Reddit for accounts and hosting. Your
use of Reddit is also governed by the
[Reddit User Agreement](https://www.redditinc.com/policies/user-agreement) and the
[Reddit Privacy Policy](https://www.reddit.com/policies/privacy-policy).

## Contact

Questions about data handling: reach the moderators of **r/OceanDriveRacing**. This
policy may be updated as the app evolves; material changes will be noted in the
subreddit.
