# WealthLab — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

## What We Collect

WealthLab is designed to keep your data on your device. The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use it.

The information saved by the app — your name, onboarding answers, lesson progress, streak, XP totals, daily goal, and saved slides — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData and UserDefaults frameworks. We do not transmit this information to any server we operate.

## Subscriptions

If you purchase a WealthLab Honors subscription, the transaction is processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat (revenuecat.com) to manage subscription receipts, validate entitlements, and let you restore purchases. RevenueCat receives a randomly-generated anonymous identifier from your device along with the App Store transaction; it does not receive your name, Apple ID email, or any of your in-app progress. Their privacy policy is available at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

## Notifications

If you opt in to daily reminders during onboarding, the app schedules local notifications on your device. No push notification service is used, and no notification data leaves your device.

## Analytics and Tracking

WealthLab does not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or cross-app tracking technologies. We do not implement Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt because we have nothing to track.

## Network Access

The app fetches no remote content. It uses the network only to validate your Honors subscription with RevenueCat and Apple's App Store. No personal data is sent during these requests beyond what Apple and RevenueCat require to verify your transaction.

## Educational Content, Not Advice

WealthLab provides general financial and business education for informational purposes only. It is not personalized financial, investment, tax, legal, accounting, or insurance advice. See the in-app Legal & Disclaimers page or our Terms of Service for the full disclaimer.

## Children's Privacy

WealthLab is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information, please contact us so we can address it.

## Your Rights

Because we do not maintain a server-side copy of your data, you can exercise the equivalent of a "delete my data" request at any time by using **Settings → Reset progress** in the app or by uninstalling WealthLab from your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated date.
